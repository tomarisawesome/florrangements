About Us

Thank you for visiting our page. Here is our story :)

WHO WE ARE:

Here at Florrangements, we are a women-owned, sustainable florist that focuses on ingenuity, personality, and quality. All of our flowers are grown from local sources. We promise the utmost delivery and care with each order.



OUR STORE:

Located in the heart of Medford, Florrangements has been serving our community since 2025. Our boutique florist shop is a cozy, welcoming space where creativity blooms. We specialize in handcrafted arrangements using locally-sourced, sustainable flowers, ensuring that every bouquet reflects both quality and care. Whether you’re stopping by to pick up a custom bouquet or arranging a special delivery, we’re dedicated to making every floral experience memorable.



OUR TEAM:

Our passionate team of florists brings creativity, expertise, and a love for flowers to every arrangement. From designing custom bouquets to ensuring sustainable sourcing, we work together to make each floral experience personal, beautiful, and memorable.
